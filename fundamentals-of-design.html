Fundamentals of Design

Project Overview

In this project, I embarked on my first journey into fundamentals of design using Adobe Illustrator to create concept sketches for the website's layout. As a beginner, I initially struggled with understanding design principles and found the process challenging. Through persistent practice and learning, I gradually developed skills in planning the structure, balance, and hierarchy of elements. This project was a crucial learning experience that helped me improve my design capabilities, teaching me how to enhance usability and visual appeal step by step. I learned that design is an iterative process, and my first attempts were far from perfect, but each iteration brought me closer to creating a more intuitive and aesthetically pleasing layout.



Key Learning Outcomes

  • Understanding design composition
  • Mastering layout structure
  • Developing visual hierarchy
  • Iterative design process
