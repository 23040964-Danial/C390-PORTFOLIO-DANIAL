Immersive Technology

Project Overview

In this module, I delved into the world of immersive technologies, including augmented reality (AR) and virtual reality (VR), which proved to be a fascinating and engaging experience. Using tools like Unity and Unreal Engine, I learned to create interactive 3D environments and design spatial interfaces. The process of crafting intuitive immersive experiences captivated me, sparking a genuine interest in this emerging field.



Key Learning Outcomes

  • Understanding spatial design principles for AR and VR environments
  • Developing user-centered designs for immersive experiences
  • Optimizing content placement within users' field of view for better ergonomics
